My team has collaborated to develop a global optimization strategy by creating content that can be used in multiple lines of business with the same website. Our "create once, deploy many" methodology has revolutionized how we develop training programs.

By creating a library of shareable content, we have been able to complete a three-week training program from scratch in less than a month. We have achieved a 68% shared content ratio across 14 lines of business, resulting in quicker new hire training program creation and allowing us to focus on engaging and impactful content driven by KPI results. Additionally, we have reduced our maintenance overhead by 50%.

Learn, Do, & Engage Strategy

  • Learn — indepent study online content.
  • Do — on-the-job observation.
  • Engage — virtual leader led session.

Utilizing an Initial Training strategy enables a comprehensive training program where learners can learn at their own pace, attend guided sessions with an instructor, and gain hands-on experience with a peer on the sales floor. Combining this approach with an optimized and global content library allows for more extensive content and faster development times.
